NSF would like to ensure that a diverse range of attendees are present at each workshop, therefore a short application process is necessary. If selected, all travel and lodging accommodations will be arranged for attendees by UCAR. Please review the details below for an overview of the application goals and process:

  1. There will be 3 face-to face workshops around the country. Exact locations have not been determined at present.
  2. There will also be 1 virtual workshop.
  3. All workshops will take place concurrently on September 26-28. Full participation is required.
  4. Each workshop will host approximately 100 attendees.
  5. Invitation to attend is required. Please apply for an invitation here. A list of application questions follows below.
  6. The application deadline is midnight (local time) August 12, 2018.
  7. The review process will take approximately 3 weeks and invitations to attend will be sent on August 27, 2018 to successful applicants.

Application Questions:

  • Email address
  • First name
  • Last name
  • Job title
  • Institution name
  • Department
  • Address
  • City
  • State or territory
  • Postal code
  • Institution type
  • Is your institution an HBCU, MSI, or Tribal College?
  • Sex/Gender
  • Career level
  • Year of terminal degree
  • Primary area of expertise
  • Other areas of expertise
  • Workshop location selections
  • Top choice for workshop location
  • Confirmation of full participation
  • Why do you want to attend a scoping session? What do you hope to contribute? What do you hope to gain? (<250 words)
